====================================================
VISION INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
====================================================

The Vision Intelligence Platform provides the perception capabilities of the Enterprise AI Copilot.

It transforms raw visual data into structured observations that can be used by the Reasoning Engine.

The platform must support real-time and offline analysis.

SECTION 15 — SCENE & BEHAVIOR UNDERSTANDING
====================================================
SCENE UNDERSTANDING
====================================================

The assistant must describe the environment.

Examples:

Loading activity

Waiting line

Crowded entrance

Vehicle congestion

Empty hallway

Maintenance work

Restricted area occupancy

====================================================
BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS
====================================================

Recognize observable behaviors such as:

Running

Loitering

Queue Formation

Object Left Behind

Object Removed

Restricted Area Entry

Wrong Direction

Unsafe Movement

Crowd Formation

====================================================
IMPORTANT
====================================================

The assistant must describe only behaviors directly observable in the video.

It must not infer emotions, intentions, criminality, or other unobservable mental states.

If multiple explanations are possible, present them as possibilities rather than facts.
